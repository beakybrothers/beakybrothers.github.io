The Beaky Brothers were created in 2019 by Jake and Simon Morgan.

Jake is starting to write books about them and making a series.

There are four Beaky Brothers:

Cheeky Beaky is cheeky by name and cheeky by nature.

Squeaky Beaky, as you can guess is always squeaking.

Sneaky Beaky, as he is called, is sneaking about all the time and scaring the others.

Beaky Beaky is the boss of the group.
